FAQs

What’s included in the price?

Design, development, basic SEO and analytics setup, and coordination to launch. Hosting/domain are billed separately, or we can help set them up on your preferred provider (e.g., Truehost, Vercel).

Can we add features later?

Yes. Start with the smallest scope that delivers value, then iterate. You can upgrade tiers or add Care time as needed.

How are payments structured?

Typically 50% to start, 25% mid‑project, 25% on launch. Care plans are prepaid monthly or yearly.

Do you offer refunds?

If something isn’t working, we’ll make it right. For prepaid Care time that isn’t used, we can roll it forward or credit it.
