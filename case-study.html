The challenge

Students needed a safe, simple way to trade items on campus and quickly find study materials. Existing options were scattered across social media and chat groups, with no central catalog, poor search, and no quality control.

Our approach

  • Designed a clear browse → product → chat checkout flow.
  • Implemented product listing with images, tags, and search.
  • Added a lightweight checkout via WhatsApp for quick conversion.
  • Built a content area for study resources (notes, past questions).
  • Optimized for mobile first; many students are on low‑data plans.
  • Performance budget: small images, lazy‑loading, caching.
  • Admin tools: approve listings, moderate content, analytics.
  • Clear governance rules and reporting for safety.
  • SEO basics: clean URLs, metadata, share images.
