Jabu Market

Student marketplace & study hub for the Joseph Ayo Babalola University (JABU) community — fast catalog + simple posting + clean growth path.

Visit site ↗ Start a similar project

Overview

We designed and built a lean marketplace that lets students list and buy items quickly (books, gadgets, essentials) and discover campus resources. The v1 focused on speed and simplicity with a clear path to expand: verified sellers, payments, and an AI‑powered study hub.

Problem

Students struggled to find trusted buyers/sellers and organized study resources in one place. Existing channels were chat‑based and noisy; deals were missed and content was scattered.

Solution

  • Fast listing flow (title, price, contact, photos) — no account required for v1.
  • Browse by category with search + campus‑friendly filters.
  • Simple moderation dashboard; email/WhatsApp contact handoff.
  • Foundation for AI Study Hub (Q&A, practice, explanations).

Results

  • MVP shipped in ~2 weeks; first listings created day 1.
  • Zero‑friction posting increased supply; moderation kept quality.
  • Clear roadmap to payments, seller verification, and AI modules.

Tech & architecture

  • Frontend: semantic HTML, modern CSS, small vanilla JS modules.
  • Backend: PHP + MySQL, REST endpoints for listings and moderation.
  • Images: server‑side resizing + responsive client `srcset`/lazy‑load.
  • Hosting: simple PHP host (e.g., Truehost) with caching & backups.
